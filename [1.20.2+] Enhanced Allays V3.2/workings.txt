- Teleports allays to the nearest player if they are 64 blocks away from them, unless nametaged
- Teleports allays to their noteblock if they are outside the 16m range and their timer runs out, unless nametaged
  - handy in watery environments where allays are slower
  - has suffication protection by teleporting them on top of the noteblock (there is always air above working noteblocks)
  - has the following leash functionality to counter this noteblock feature
- leashed allays cannot like noteblocks and thus follow the player
- naming an allay "unname" causes it lose its customname
- /trigger invulnerable_allays  makes your allays immortal
  - trigger again to make them mortal again
